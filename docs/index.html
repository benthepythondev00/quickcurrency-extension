Privacy Policy

QuickCurrency Chrome Extension • Last Updated: December 2025

Overview

QuickCurrency ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that helps you convert currencies quickly with real-time exchange rates. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

Key Point: All your preferences and conversion history are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to your personal information.

Data Collection and Storage

What Data We Store

The Extension stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Your preferred currencies (from/to)
  • Recent currency selections
  • Conversion history
  • App settings

How Data is Stored

All user data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.local). Your data:

  • Never leaves your browser
  • Is not sent to any external servers (except for exchange rates)
  • Is not shared with any third parties
  • Is not accessible to us or anyone else

Exchange Rate Data

To provide currency conversion, the Extension fetches exchange rate data from a public API:

  • We use the fawazahmed0/exchange-api - a free, open-source currency API
  • Only exchange rate data is fetched - no personal information is sent
  • The API does not track users or collect any data

Data You Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • View your conversion history in the Extension
  • Clear history at any time
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data

Permissions We Request

The Extension requests the following permissions:

  1. storage: To save your preferences and conversion history locally

We request minimal permissions to ensure your privacy and security.

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Collect any analytics or usage data
  • Send any personal data to external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Access any websites you visit

Third-Party Services

The Extension uses a public currency exchange rate API to fetch current rates. This API:

  • Is open-source and free
  • Does not require authentication
  • Does not collect user data
  • Only provides exchange rate information

Children's Privacy

QuickCurrency is suitable for users of all ages. We do not collect any personal data from any users, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we have no access to it, you can exercise all data rights directly through the Extension's interface or by uninstalling the extension.
